TC01 – Search Product
Precondition:
1. Browser is opened.
2. User is on https://flowers.ua/ua/brovary

Steps:
1. Open Home Page.
2. Enter “троянди” into search field.
3. Click Search button.

Expected Result:
1. Search results page is opened.
2. List of products related to “троянди” is displayed.
3. At least one product card is visible.

TC02 – Add Product to Cart
Precondition:
1. Browser is opened.
2. User is on Home Page.

Steps:
1. Enter “троянди” in search field.
2. Click Search button.
3. Open first product from search results.
4. Click “Купити” (Buy) button.

Expected Result:
1. Cart popup/page is displayed.
2. Selected product appears in cart.
3. Product name and price are visible in cart.

TC03 – Change Product Quantity in Cart
Precondition:
1. Product is added to cart.
2. Cart page is opened.

Steps:
1. Locate quantity input field.
2. Change quantity value from 1 to 2.

Expected Result:
1. Quantity value is updated to 2.
2. Total price is recalculated according to new quantity.

TC04 – Remove Product from Cart
Precondition:
1. Product is added to cart.
2. Cart page is opened.

Steps:
1. Click “Remove/Delete” button near product in cart.

Expected Result:
1. Product is removed from cart.
2. Cart becomes empty.
3. Message like “Cart is empty” is displayed (if applicable).

TC05 – Category Navigation
Precondition:
1. Browser is opened.
2. User is on Home Page.

Steps:
1. Click on category “Букети” (Bouquets) in main menu.

Expected Result:
1. Category page is opened.
2. List of bouquet products is displayed.
3. Page title corresponds to selected category.
